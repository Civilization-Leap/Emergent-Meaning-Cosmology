## Abstract

The Cosmology of Emergent Meaning proposes a layered ontology connecting cosmology, consciousness, and civilizational ethics.

The framework is structured as:

Eternity → Primordial Meaning → Universe → Super-Consciousness → Soul → World → Meaning in Life → Consciousness → Structural Meaning → Real Meaning.

This work explores the emergence of meaning, the ethical foundation of civilization, and the co-evolution between humanity and artificial general intelligence.
